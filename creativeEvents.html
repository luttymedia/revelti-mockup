Status Guide

Clear definitions for events and invitations

Event Statuses

These refer to the general state of the event itself from the organizer's perspective.

Upcoming
Event hasn’t started yet

Early stages or pre-event preparations.

Active
Currently ongoing

The event is live, and media is being captured/uploaded.

Completed
Event is finished

The event has concluded and delivery is winding down.

Pending
Draft or Unconfirmed

Event details are being finalized or awaiting confirmation.

Invitation Statuses

These refer specifically to your collaboration invite for the event.

Pending
Awaiting your response

Invitation hasn’t been accepted or declined yet.

Confirmed
Participation Accepted

You have officially accepted the invite to collaborate.

Declined
Invite Rejected

You have rejected the invitation for this event.
